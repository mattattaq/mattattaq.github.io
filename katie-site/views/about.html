Sole Seoul Soul

“Sole Seoul Soul” tells the story of a suidice victim, Ileum who becomes a reaper after dying. He can't remember his past life and therfore cannot pass on without reaping souls. When he coes to reap his first soul: Joon, Joon screams. Ileum assumes that Joon is afraid of him and runs away before Joon can talk to him. He looks into the mirror and sees the reflection of a monster. The head reaper Deva comes and suggests trying on the shoes of the other passed souls. This changes his appearance so he can be more relatable to the souls he is reaping. However, what Ileum doesn't know is that Joon knew him when they were alive and screamed out of surprise.

About Kathryn

Kathryn Silver is a narrative artist who visually features characters that cope with mental health and academic disabilities in magical realistic settings. She will continue creating comics traditional and digitally to participate actively in the movement to change and define what living with mental health diagnosis means in the eye of the public through visual storytelling. Kathryn hopes to diversify student characters media shows with mental health diagnosis and disability and to spread the knowledge of rights that everyone is entitled to. Kathryn graduated from SCAD in 2012 with a degree in Sequential Art. Enamored by color, she worked for Hilary Sycamore on books for First Second. From 2015-2017 she self published a few short comics, “Witch Amy” “AHA” and “Hope Retirement Home”. Wanting to pursue storytelling through more than color and hone her craft She attends MFA Visual Narrative at SVA in hopes to clarify the combination of her concepts and writing with illustration.
